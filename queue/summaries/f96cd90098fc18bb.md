OpenAI, Oracle, and SoftBank have jointly announced the launch of five new Stargate AI datacenter sites across the United States. This initiative aims to accelerate a massive $500 billion infrastructure investment focused on deploying 10 gigawatts of power to support next-generation artificial intelligence technologies. The project is expected to significantly boost the U.S. AI ecosystem by enhancing computational capacity and will create tens of thousands of new jobs, contributing to economic growth in the technology sector and related industries.
