  The RFC aims to gather feedback from stakeholders—including industry, academia, civil society, and the public—on how accountability can be defined, implemented, and upheld in AI systems.

  The request explores topics such as transparency, oversight, redress mechanisms, auditability, and governance structures that ensure AI systems operate fairly and ethically.

- **Policy Goals:**  
  To help shape policies that encourage innovation while protecting users from risks like bias, misinformation, unfair discrimination, and privacy violations.

- **Who is invited to comment:**  
  The NTIA welcomes input from developers, users, researchers, advocacy groups, and policymakers.

  Comments can typically be submitted via the Federal Register or the NTIA website, following instructions provided in the official notice.
