"American-made innovation" refers to inventions, technologies, products, and advancements that have been developed within the United States. Throughout history, the U.S. has been a global leader in innovation across a wide variety of fields such as technology, medicine, transportation, and entertainment.

Some notable examples of American-made innovations include:

- **The airplane**: The Wright brothers’ first powered flight in 1903.
- **The light bulb**: Thomas Edison’s development and commercialization of practical electric lighting.
- **The internet**: Developed from ARPANET, funded by the U.S. Department of Defense.
- **Personal computers**: Innovations by companies like IBM, Apple, and Microsoft.
- **Space exploration**: NASA’s Apollo missions, including the first moon landing.
- **Medical advancements**: Development of vaccines, MRI technology, and more.
