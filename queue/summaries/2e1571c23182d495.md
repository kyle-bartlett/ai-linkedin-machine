Samsung and SK have teamed up with OpenAI in the Stargate initiative to enhance global AI infrastructure. This collaboration focuses on scaling the production of advanced memory chips and constructing next-generation data centers in South Korea. By leveraging Samsung's and SK's semiconductor manufacturing capabilities along with OpenAI's AI expertise, the partnership aims to support the growing demand for AI compute power and accelerate AI development worldwide. This move highlights a strategic effort to strengthen South Korea's position in the global AI hardware ecosystem and foster innovation in AI technology infrastructure.
