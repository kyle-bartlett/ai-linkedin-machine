OpenAI’s new Expert Council on Well-Being and AI is a team of top psychologists, clinicians, and researchers dedicated to ensuring that ChatGPT supports emotional health in a safe and compassionate way—especially for teenagers. By combining their expertise with AI development, the council helps shape how ChatGPT responds to users experiencing emotional challenges, promotes positive mental health, and navigates sensitive topics with care. Their insights guide the design of AI interactions that prioritize empathy, understanding, and safety, making ChatGPT a more supportive tool for emotional well-being across diverse user communities.
