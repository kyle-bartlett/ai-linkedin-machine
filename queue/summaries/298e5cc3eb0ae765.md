### 1. Conceptualize Your Story Arc

- The mystical forest discovery  
- Encounter with mythical creatures  
- The hero’s challenge at the ancient ruins  
- The climactic battle  
- The peaceful resolution

### 2. Create Individual Scenes or a Continuous Mural  
- **Individual Scene Images:** Generate detailed images for each key moment. You can specify the size based on the complexity, e.g., a large 1024x1024 for epic battles and smaller sizes for intimate character moments.
