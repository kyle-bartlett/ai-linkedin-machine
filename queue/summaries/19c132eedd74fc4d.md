OpenAI and Future, the global platform for specialist media, have today announced a strategic partnership to bring content from Future’s 200-plus media brands to OpenAI’s users.

This collaboration aims to integrate Future’s extensive range of expert content across various sectors—including technology, gaming, music, photography, and more—into OpenAI’s services. By doing so, OpenAI users will gain enhanced access to high-quality, up-to-date information and insights from leading specialist publications.

The partnership reflects a broader trend of combining AI capabilities with authoritative content creators to deliver richer, more reliable, and context-aware information to users worldwide.
